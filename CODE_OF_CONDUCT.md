# Code of Conduct

We are here to build software. That is the only purpose of this space.

## Standards

- Judge contributions on technical merit.
- Be direct. Disagreement is fine. Hostility is not.
- Criticize the work, not the person.
- Respect people's time. Be specific and on topic.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment of any kind - public or private.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Personal attacks, insults, or threats.
- Publishing someone's private information without consent.
- Sustained hostility, even if each individual comment seems minor.

**No exceptions.** Not for students, not for new contributors, not for age,
not for "I didn't know." The rules are here. You are reading them.

One confirmed harassment report means permanent removal. No warnings. No
appeals.

## Bad-Faith Participation

**Mocking.** Mocking issue titles, PR names, design decisions, variable names,
or any part of someone's work. If you have a technical objection, state it.
If you are here to ridicule, leave.

**Drive-by dismissals.** "Skill issue." "Just use X." "Why does this exist."
These add nothing. They will be deleted and the person warned once.

**Unsolicited rewrites.** Telling the project to change its language, runtime,
or architecture when you have not contributed is not feedback. It is noise.

**Derailing.** Taking an issue or PR thread off-topic into politics, ideology,
or anything unrelated to the technical matter at hand. Comments will be
removed.

## Prohibited Agendas

**Challenging this document.** Opening an issue to argue against this CoC is
not a valid contribution. Close the tab.

**License campaigns.** Pushing license changes without prior contribution is
bad faith. Genuine legal concerns go to the maintainers privately.

**Language wars.** This project is written in Zig. That is not changing.

## Coordinated Action

Organizing external pressure against this project, its maintainers, or
contributors - through social media, group chats, or anywhere else - is a
violation. Everyone identified as part of coordinated action will be
permanently removed without individual warnings.

## Evasion and Report Abuse

**Ban evasion.** Coming back on an alt after being removed will be documented
and reported to the platform.

**False reports.** Filing a harassment report as a weapon against someone you
dislike is itself harassment. It is subject to the same immediate permanent
removal standard — no warnings, no appeals.

## AI Policy

AI tools are allowed here as productivity aids. That is not up for debate.

**What is not allowed:**

Submitting AI-generated content you have not read, tested, or taken ownership
of. You are responsible for everything you submit. "The AI wrote it" is not
an excuse for a bug or a broken PR. Reviewers are not your cleanup crew.

Coming here to push an agenda about AI - for or against. This means: opening
issues to argue that AI should be banned, policing whether someone used AI
assistance, leaving comments about AI ethics or harm, calling contributions
"AI slop" without a specific technical complaint, or writing opinion pieces in
issue threads. None of that is a contribution. It will be removed.

Challenging this policy. It is not a draft. It is not open for community
revision. If you disagree, fork the project.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: the repository, issues, pull requests,
discussions, and any official channels. It also applies when someone is
visibly representing this project in external spaces.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

The fern-tui team enforces this document consistently. They can remove, edit, or
reject any contribution that violates it. They can ban anyone whose behavior
harms the project or its contributors. They do not owe explanations to people
they remove.

All enforcement actions are recorded. Past violations are considered in future
decisions.

## Enforcement

Report violations using the reporting form at <will_be_added_soon>. Reports
are confidential. The reporter's identity will not be disclosed to the
reported party. The fern-tui team acts on clear evidence without requiring
multiple witnesses. Reports will be acknowledged within 48 hours.

**Immediate permanent removal:** harassment, coordinated action, ban evasion,
false reports, any threat to a person's safety.

**Warning then permanent removal:** bad-faith participation, prohibited
agenda-pushing, repeated AI policy violations.

There is no clean slate. Prior behavior is always on record.
